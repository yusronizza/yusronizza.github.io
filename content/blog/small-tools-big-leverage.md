DeployBot took a weekend to build and saved the team a recurring 20 minutes per release, plus the much larger cost of accidental Friday deploys. That ratio is hard to beat with feature work.

Internal tools get neglected because they don't show up on a roadmap. But a tool used by five engineers ten times a day compounds faster than most user-facing features.

If you're looking for a high-leverage side project inside your team, look for the manual step everyone complains about in retro and never gets prioritized. That's usually a weekend of work away from disappearing.
